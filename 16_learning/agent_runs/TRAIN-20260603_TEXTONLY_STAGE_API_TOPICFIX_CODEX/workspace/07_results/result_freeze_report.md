### 3.2 Metric Definitions and Directions

- **R^2 (coefficient of determination):** higher is better; dimensionless; used for overall fit assessment.
- **RMSE (root mean squared error):** lower is better; unit mg/L; penalizes large errors.
- **MAE (mean absolute error):** lower is better; unit mg/L; interpretable average error.
